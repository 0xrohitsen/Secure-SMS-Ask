**Private. Secure. Reliable SMS Forwarding.**

Secure SMS Ask is a privacy-focused Android application designed to help you safely manage and forward your SMS messages. It provides you with full control over your data, ensuring your conversations remain private and secure. The app is open-source, ad-free, and built with a privacy-first philosophy.

## ‚ú® Key Features

- **Privacy First**: No data collection, no tracking, and no third-party analytics. Your privacy is our top priority.
- **Secure SMS Forwarding**: Configure a secure endpoint to forward your SMS messages reliably.
- **Runs in the Background**: A seamless automatic background service that works even after your device reboots.
- **App Lock Protection**: Secure the application with a PIN to prevent unauthorized access.
- **Open Source**: The complete source code is available on GitHub for transparency and community review.
- **Ad-Free**: A clean, distraction-free experience, forever.
- **Local Processing**: All operations happen on your device. Your data never leaves it.

## üìñ How to Use

1.  **Install the App**: Follow the installation steps above.
2.  **Grant SMS Permission**: On first launch, the app will request permission to read your SMS messages. This is necessary for it to forward them.
3.  **Configure Forwarding**: In the app's settings, set up your secure forwarding endpoint and define any rules for which messages to forward.
4.  **Runs Silently**: The app will now run silently and securely in the background, forwarding messages as they arrive.
